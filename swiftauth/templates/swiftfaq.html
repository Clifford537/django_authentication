Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button and fill in the required information.

2. How do I add a bank card?

You can add a bank card by navigating to the "Add bank Card" section and filling out the form.

3. How do I activate my account?

Click on the activation link in the alert message or go to the "Activate Account" section.

4. What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link and follow the instructions.

5. How can I view my transfer history?

Your transfer history can be viewed in the "Transfer History" section.

6. How do I remove a bank card?

To remove a bank card, navigate to the "Existing Cards" section and click on the "Remove Card" button.

7. Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use advanced security measures to protect your personal information.

8. How do I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support by clicking on the "Contact Us" link at the bottom of the page.

9. Are there any fees for using the service?

Our service has no hidden fees. You will be informed of any applicable fees upfront.

10. Can I use the service internationally?

Yes, our service can be used internationally.

11. How do I update my account information?

To update your account information, go to the "Account Settings" section.

12. What is the minimum amount I can transfer?

The minimum transfer amount is $10.

13. How long does it take for transfers to process?

Transfers typically process within 1-3 business days.

14. Can I cancel a transfer?

Once a transfer is initiated, it cannot be canceled.

15. How do I close my account?

To close your account, contact customer support for assistance.
